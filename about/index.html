My Professional Journey

The Foundation

My career began with a deep fascination for color theory and the art of transformation. I spent my early years training under master artists, learning that the most powerful beauty is often the most subtle.

02

Industry Experience

Years in the editorial and fashion world refined my technical skills. Working on high-pressure runway shows and professional sets taught me the importance of longevity, precision, and the art of skin-first makeup.

03

The Signature Style

Today, I've synthesized my experiences into a signature approach focused on timeless elegance. My mission is to provide an elevated, personalized experience that honors each client's unique individuality.
